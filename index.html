Frequently asked questions

How do I pay with Vipps?

At checkout, tap “Pay in Vipps”. We’ll help open the Vipps app and we copy the number (458 98 908) and the amount. Complete the payment in Vipps, then tap “Confirm order” to send us your order as an SMS.

Do you offer pickup or cash?

Yes. Choose “Pickup and pay cash” at checkout. We’ll contact you by email to arrange the time and place.

What about returns and exchanges?

Unused items can be returned within 14 days. For hygiene reasons, earrings must be unopened in original packaging. Email us at thejhumkaluxury@gmail.com to arrange a return.

Shipping time

Domestic delivery typically 2–3 weeks based on availability. Pickup can be arranged faster depending on availability.
